Our Services

Explore how Al-Zahraa Centre serves the community through worship, programmes and support.

Current Services

These are the main services currently being offered at the centre. For updates and timings, please see our events calendar or contact us.

Prayer Facilities

Dedicated prayer spaces with separate areas for men and women, providing a calm environment for daily worship and programmes.

Supporting These Services

Al-Zahraa Centre is entirely community-funded. Every programme, bill and improvement is made possible by the generosity of those who attend and care about having a Shia centre in Sussex.

  • Maintaining and improving prayer spaces
  • Covering rent, utilities and essential running costs
  • Hosting regular programmes and community gatherings
  • Developing new educational and pastoral services

Regular monthly donations, even small amounts, help us plan sustainably and serve the community better.

For questions about donations or alternative ways to contribute (e.g. bank transfer), please see our Donations page.
