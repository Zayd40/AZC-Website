Volunteer with Us

Join our community of volunteers and help grow Sussex’s only Shia Islamic centre.

Why Your Help Matters

Al-Zahraa Centre is run almost entirely by volunteers. Every majlis, iftar, children’s programme and community gathering depends on people like you who are willing to give their time and skills for the sake of Allah (SWT).

Whether you can offer regular help or just support during busy periods, you’ll be contributing to something that benefits families, students and visitors across Brighton & Hove and beyond.

What You’ll Gain

  • Opportunities to serve the community for the sake of Allah (SWT)
  • Skills in event organisation, teamwork and communication
  • Friendships with like-minded community members
  • Experience that can support your personal and professional growth

Who We’re Looking For

You don’t need to be an expert or have years of experience. We’re mainly looking for people who care about the community and are willing to show up consistently.

Qualities We Value

  • A sincere intention to serve the community
  • Reliability and good communication
  • Respect for Islamic values and diversity
  • A willingness to work as part of a team

Practical Requirements

  • Minimum age of 16 (with parental consent if under 18)
  • Able to offer at least a few hours per month
  • Basic English communication
  • Attendance at a short orientation or briefing
  • DBS checks may be required for certain roles (e.g. youth work)

No previous experience is required. We’ll guide you, pair you with experienced volunteers and support you as you learn.
