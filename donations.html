Support Our Centre

Your generosity keeps Sussex’s only Shia Islamic centre open, active and welcoming.

Community-Funded, Community-Run

Al-Zahraa Centre is a non-profit, community-funded organisation. We do not receive government funding or large institutional grants. Every bill paid, programme delivered and improvement made is only possible because individuals and families choose to support the centre.

Your donations allow us to provide a dedicated Shia space for worship, learning and community in Brighton & Hove, accessible to people from all backgrounds and stages of life.

Where Your Donation Goes

  • Rent, utilities and insurance for the building
  • Religious programmes (Muharram, Ramadan, Eid, wiladah & shahadah occasions)
  • Community meals and hospitality
  • Educational and youth activities
  • Helping families in need through community support
  • Essential supplies and ongoing maintenance

Every amount helps. Small, regular donations are especially powerful because they allow us to plan and serve the community consistently.

Our Monthly Needs

To be transparent, here is a simplified view of our typical monthly costs. These figures are approximate and may vary.

Facility Costs

Rent, utilities, insurance and basic maintenance to keep the doors open and the centre safe.

Approx. £800 / month

Programmes & Events

Religious programmes, guest speakers, educational activities and community gatherings.

Approx. £300 / month

Community Meals

Food for iftar, programme refreshments and hospitality for attendees and guests.

Approx. £200 / month

Educational Resources

Books, teaching materials and equipment for current and future educational programmes.

Approx. £100 / month

Community Support

Assisting families in need and supporting charitable initiatives where possible.

Approx. £150 / month

Maintenance & Improvements

Ongoing repairs, cleaning and improvements to keep the centre in good condition.

Approx. £100 / month

Total Basic Monthly Need: approx. £1,650

Additional donations allow us to expand our programmes, improve facilities and respond to more community needs.

A Note of Gratitude

We are deeply grateful to everyone who has donated, volunteered or supported Al-Zahraa Centre in any way. Your contributions mean that there is a visible, vibrant Shia presence in Brighton & Hove.

  • Families have a space to worship and learn together.
  • Students and young professionals find community away from home.
  • Those exploring Islam or the path of Ahlul Bayt (AS) find a welcoming environment.
