Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions. If yours isn’t listed, feel free to reach out.

Do I need to be from a specific background to attend?

No. The centre is open to all Shia Muslims and friends of Ahlul Bayt (AS), regardless of ethnicity or background.

Are programmes in English?

Yes, our primary language is English to make programmes accessible to a wide range of attendees.

Can I bring children?

Yes, families are welcome. We ask that parents supervise their children and help us maintain the sanctity of the programmes.

How can I support the centre?

You can support by attending, volunteering, making donations and sharing information about the centre with others.
