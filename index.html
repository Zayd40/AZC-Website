A home for Shia Muslims across Sussex

Al-Zahraa Centre is a community-funded Shia Islamic centre serving Brighton, Hove and the wider Sussex area. Our programmes are delivered primarily in English, making the centre accessible to second- and third-generation community members, converts, and seekers who may not speak other community languages fluently.

We aim to be a welcoming space for worship, learning, service, and meaningful connection for everyone seeking to grow spiritually and morally.

Daily worship & observances

Separate prayer spaces for men and women, with programmes for key dates in the Islamic calendar.

Community & belonging

Regular gatherings, social activities, and a supportive environment for families, youth, and individuals.

Learning & growth

Talks, lectures, and developing educational programmes rooted in the teachings of Ahlul Bayt (AS).
